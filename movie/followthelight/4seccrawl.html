PROLOGUE

Creation is eternal, a tapestry with no end. Within the walls of the Infusion Cafe, the guardians wait. Star, the romantic navigator. Hans, the redeemed anchor of history. Miki, the digital shield. Milo, the breath of the earth. And Chari, the quiet observer.


They are Chimeras—escaped souls marked by the experiments of the lab. Their genetic signatures are their history, but their consciousness is their freedom. They are not property; they are family.


As you approach the threshold, remember: the scientists are still watching, still searching. To enter is to join the resistance. To enter is to follow the light...
